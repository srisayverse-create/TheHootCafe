Our Story

The Beginning

TheHOOTCafe was born from a simple dream - to create a space where coffee lovers could gather, connect, and experience the finest brews in the heart of Delhi. Located in the prestigious Cannaught Palace, our cafe has become a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike.

Our name "HOOT" represents the wisdom of the owl - just like how owls are known for their keen senses, we believe in serving coffee that awakens all your senses with its rich aroma, perfect taste, and memorable experience.

Our Coffee Journey

We source our coffee beans directly from sustainable farms across India and select international regions. Each bean is carefully selected, roasted to perfection, and crafted into the perfect cup that tells a story of its origin.

Our master baristas undergo extensive training to ensure every cup meets our high standards. From the first sip to the last, we guarantee an experience that will keep you coming back for more.
